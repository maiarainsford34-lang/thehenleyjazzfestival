Producer / Artistic Director
Rebecca Poole
Producer / Artistic Director

Rebecca
Poole

Rebecca has had an illustrious music career, supporting Jools Holland on tour with live performances at the Royal Albert Hall and Simply Red at the London Palladium. The acclaimed singer-songwriter has performed her self-penned songs to 90,000 people at Wembley Stadium, and shared stages with Jamie Cullum and Lana Del Rey.

She appeared in hit Netflix series The Crown, singing 'Can't Take My Eyes Off You', and her latest album Dreamers Ball has garnered exceptional reviews alongside Record of the Week on BBC Sounds, Smooth Jazz, and Jazz FM — where she remains a firm fixture on the A playlist.

Rebecca's passion for jazz and blues led her to found Purdy's Pop Up at the HAODS Studio in Henley in 2017 — an intimate jazz club that has been showcasing world-class acts and selling out ever since. A devoted advocate of live music and community events, she is thrilled to put The Henley Jazz Festival firmly on the map.

Producer / Artistic Director
Denny Ilett
Producer / Artistic Director

Denny
Ilett

Denny Ilett is a musician, bandleader, and arts organiser whose career spans jazz, blues, big band, rock, soul, funk, and beyond. As artistic director of The Henley Jazz Festival, Denny brings world-class programming and decades of experience at the heart of the music world to one of the Thames Valley's most exciting new cultural events.

His vision is shaped by a remarkable performing career — collaborations with funk legend Pee Wee Ellis, conductor Charles Hazlewood, Will Gregory (Goldfrapp), Adrian Utley (Portishead), Paul Jones (Manfred Mann), trumpet virtuoso Bobby Shew, and New Orleans vocalist Lillian Boutté, with whom he toured globally for over two decades as musical director.

"A knockout guitarist, soulful and jazz-tinged."

— Guitarist Magazine

Denny co-founded the Bristol Jazz and Blues Festival in 2013, where as artistic director he brought Dr. John, Maceo Parker, Madeleine Peyroux, Lee Konitz, and John Scofield to the city. A contributor to Guitarist magazine and London Jazz News, and founder of the acclaimed Electric Lady Big Band, he is an artist who understands music from every angle — as performer, curator, and champion of live jazz.
