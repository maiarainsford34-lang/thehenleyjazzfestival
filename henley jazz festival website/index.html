Welcome

Henley's first jazz festival

We are thrilled to bring you a packed weekend of live music from some of the UK's finest jazz and blues musicians — a timeless golden era, brought back to life on the banks of the Thames.

From an authentic trad-jazz trip aboard the New Orleans Paddle Steamer, swing and Latin dancing to a big band at the Town Hall, the greatest entertainers at the Kenton Theatre, the speakeasy at Purdy's Pop Up, or sipping wine in the musical vines at Oakengrove Vineyard — there is something for everyone.

Find your favourites, grab your tickets, and we'll see you there.
